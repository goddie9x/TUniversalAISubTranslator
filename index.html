Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 11, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how TUniversal AI Sub Translator ("the Extension") handles user data. We value your privacy and are committed to being transparent about our data practices.

1. Data Collection and Usage

TUniversal AI Sub Translator is designed with a "Privacy-First" approach. We do not collect, store, or transmit any of your personal information to our own servers.

  • API Keys: Your Gemini or Mistral API keys are stored locally on your device using chrome.storage.local. These keys are only used to authenticate requests to the respective AI providers.
  • Subtitle Content: The extension captures subtitle text from the websites you visit solely for the purpose of translation. This text is sent directly to the AI provider (Google or Mistral) you have configured.
  • Settings and Preferences: Your UI preferences (font size, colors, position) are stored locally on your machine.

2. Third-Party Service Providers

When you use the translation feature, the subtitle text is sent to third-party AI providers. Please refer to their respective privacy policies for details on how they handle data:

  • Google Privacy Policy (for Gemini API)
  • Mistral AI Privacy Policy

3. Data Security

Since all sensitive information (like API Keys) is stored locally in your browser's secure storage, the security of your data depends on the security of your device. We recommend not sharing your API keys with anyone.

4. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Effective Date" at the top.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us through the official support channel on the Chrome Web Store.
